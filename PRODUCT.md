# PRODUCT.md — oSign.EU

register: product

## Product purpose
oSign.EU is an eIDAS-oriented electronic signature platform. Businesses build
reusable **contract templates**, personalize them with **variables** (issuer/account
data: company name, NIF, address…) and **dynamic fields** (signer data captured at
sign time: name, NIF, plus custom fields), then collect legally-valid signatures by
email, SMS, on-screen (local), tablet or QR, with a tamper-evident audit trail.
It also powers partner integrations (e.g. the mivet/VetControl/SaludControl apps).

## Users
Small and medium business operators, clinics, and professionals in Spain/EU. Mostly
**non-technical**: they write everyday contracts (LOPD consent, service agreements)
and need to send them for signature in seconds, often from a phone between tasks.
They are not lawyers and not engineers; the tool must feel approachable, fast, and
trustworthy without legal jargon.

## Brand & tone
Trustworthy, precise, efficient. Compliance-grade confidence without intimidation.
Plain Spanish, short labels, no legalese. The product gets out of the way: the
content of the contract is the hero, the chrome is quiet.

## Strategic principles
- **Mobile-first reality.** Operators use this on phones and tablets as much as
  desktop. Every authoring surface must be fully usable at 375px wide.
- **The document is the focus.** Editing chrome (toolbars, tabs, field palettes)
  supports the text; it never competes with it.
- **Progressive disclosure.** Advanced options (AI generation, parameters, raw code)
  are available but never block the simple path: name it, write it, add fields, save.
- **Legibility of field types.** Variables (issuer) vs dynamic fields (signer) must be
  instantly distinguishable, and consistently so across editor, palette and preview.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered enterprise CLM suites with dense ribbons and modal-on-modal flows.
- Legalese-heavy interfaces that intimidate non-lawyers.
- Toolbars that overflow or break on mobile.
